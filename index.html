Meet the fun creators!

The first night Alyssa and Daniel met, they instantly bonded over Catan cupcakes and the card game Exploding Kittens. Since then, they have fangirled over their favorite fandoms and their love of baking. With a similar background in customer service roles, the dynamic duo have a passion for serving others and creating magical moments.

As such it became a no-brainer to fuse both ideas of baking and their love of fandoms into an effective business of making delicious treats that would appeal to anyone and everyone.
